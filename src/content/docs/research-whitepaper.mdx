The canonical zkmcu paper. Pulls together the [prior-art survey](/research-prior-art/), [audit](/audit-poseidon2/), [security model](/security/), [self-audit](/self-audit/), and on-silicon measurements into one citable document. Pre-print, not submitted to a venue yet, plan is RWC or USENIX once Phase 6 (PQ-Semaphore) lands.

## What's in it

- **Threat model.** Who's attacking, what they control, what zkmcu defends against. Goes deeper than the [security page](/security/).
- **System architecture.** Three verifier crates (BN254 / BLS12-381 / STARK), shared parser shape, allocator strategy, the bump-allocator and TLSF allocator deltas, firmware bench harness.
- **Measurements.** Every published number with measurement methodology, hardware setup, build flags. The [benchmarks page](/benchmarks/) is the digest, the whitepaper is the full data.
- **Security analysis.** Adversarial test methodology, parser hardening, the canonical-encoding strictness (BN254 `Fr < r`, BLS12-381 padding validation), DoS hardening. Cross-references the [self-audit](/self-audit/) and [Poseidon2 audit](/audit-poseidon2/).
- **Limitations.** Honest list of what isn't validated yet (constant-time formal proof, side-channel via power / EM, upstream subgroup math).
- **Roadmap context.** Why phases 6-9 ([Roadmap](/roadmap/)) follow the order they do.
